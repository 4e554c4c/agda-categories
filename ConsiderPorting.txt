Below is a list of all of the .agda files in the copumpkin library. They should be classified
into 'done', 'consider' and 'drop'; 'part' may be worth considering, for those files where
only part has been done. Only put in 'done' when everything that will eventually be ported has
actually been.

Note that the names in the new are not always going to be the same!

Under 'consider', it is worth putting in a line or two of 'documentation' regarding
what is actually defined in that file (when it's not obvious). It is also worth moving
the stuff around in a rough priority order (higher priority at the top) for the benefit
of potential contributors.
